Asylum

Madeleine Roux

Once you get in, there's no getting out

Summary

This book follows a boy named Dan Crawford, who is going to attend a prestigious college preparatory summer program. There, he meets two other teens, who then explore an old mental hospital that has been re-purposed into a student dormitory. Together, they uncover the dark past of the building.

Chapters 1–5

These chapters set the scene for the rest of the book. We get a description of the college and asylum as well as our main character, Dan, and other characters. The story starts slowly as Dan’s roommate mentions the creepy old offices, sparking Dan’s curiosity.

After

This book uses real photographs that help visualize certain scenes and add to the unsettling atmosphere. The images are creepy and effective. The story is full of twists and turns, which makes it especially fun and unpredictable.
