Summary

This book follows a boy namead Dan Crawford, who is going to attend a presteigous college preparatory summer program. There, he meets two other teens, who then explore an old mental hospital that has been re-purposed into a student dormitory. They then uncover a dark past of the building.

Chapters 1-5

In this chapter we set the scene for the rest of the book. we get a description of the college/ asylum as well as our main character, Dan; as well as some of our other main characters. The story starts a little slow with Dan's roomate being the one to mention the creepy old offices of the asylum, which Dan wants to explore.
